Introduction

As our knowledge of space grows gradually, the need to educate people on it and its mysterious properties grows too. Space Explorer was created to satisfy that need. Here at Space Explorer, our goal is to encourage interest and research in space by making information acquired over the years accessible to the public in an organized and intriguing manner.

What is Space?

Scientifically, space is defined as a three-dimensional continuum containing positions and directions. From that denotation and basic knowledge of space, we can assume that space is an ever-growing domain containing secrets that will most likely not be revealed in our lifetime. However, this fact should not deter us from exploring space and uncovering one secret at a time.
